Hence, following the right steps and working on the <strong>best small business ideas</strong> can help you establish your own <strong>business with little investment.</strong></p><p>The dynamics of business have changed. New <strong>business trends in 2020 </strong>have come up in<strong> </strong>and some existing ones have gained traction. This post focuses on the trends in the world of small business ideas. An exciting feature of the <strong><a href="https://startuptalky.com/tag/business-ideas/"> top small business ideas of 2020 </a></strong>discussed below is that even beginners can try them and that too with small investments. </p><p>There are over 4 billion internet users in the world, and 6 out of 10 small businesses have a website to serve these users. According to statistics, 73% of online businesses earn over $50,000 per year. Hence, most of the ideas in this post are online-oriented. Not every job requires you to be on-site. There are certain services which can be performed online without any hassle. Bookkeeping is one such example. If you have a strong foundation in the basics of income, outgoings, etc., you can make a solid income through online bookkeeping. Many people value freelancing over a day job. Be it side income or more than that, you can achieve a lot as a reputed freelancer. Sell your services at <strong>Fiverr.com and Freelancer.com</strong>. You could do voice-overs, translations, transcribing people's text, content writing, logo designing or even programming! It's all about finding the match with the perfect client. </p><p>Established freelancers earn hundreds of dollars in a day's work! Working from anywhere and at your own timeline is another perk of freelancing. So freelancing is a tried-and-tested approach of starting a small business and making it big someday. This is the best small business idea because you can follow your passion and make money out of it.</p><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "byju_ReviewingProducts"> Reviewing Products</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://startuptalky.com/content/images/2020/05/reviewing-products_startuptalky-min.jpg" class="kg-image" alt="on-demand small business ideas"><figcaption>Product Reviewing</figcaption></figure><p>Product review is an <strong>easy small business idea</strong> that can be started online with minimal investment. People like to read and watch reviews before buying products. Leverage this demand to build a small business around reviewing products. A new product has been recently launched? Procure it and upload a video on YouTube to share your review. You can also make tutorials on how to use features and functionalities of devices and other products. There are many out there who purchase fancy gadgets in a whim only to not use it the fullest. </p><hr><p><strong>Also read : </strong><a href="https://startuptalky.com/women-business-ideas/" rel="bookmark">Best Small Business Ideas for Women</a></p><hr><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "byju_AffiliateMarketers"> Affiliate Marketing - Best Small Business Idea</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>Consider the following example to understand affiliate marketing, You review a newly launched camera, make a video of your review and upload it on YouTube. You also add links in the video description. These links are special because if anyone goes to Amazon by clicking on them and buys the camera from there within 24 hours, you get a certain commission from the purchase. This is why reviewers provide a plethora of links. If you are interested in affiliate marketing, do check out the Amazon associate program and Shopify affiliate program. </p><figure class="kg-card kg-embed-card kg-card-hascaption"><iframe width="480" height="270" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/G0dzLanYW1E?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe><figcaption>Small Business Ideas Popular In 2020</figcaption></figure><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "scoopwhoop_GraphicsDesigning"> Graphic Designing</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>Content sells only when it's attractive. Logos are effective only when they are pleasing to the eye. Hence, the everlasting need for graphic designing. If you have awesome photo editing and designing skills, then graphic designing is a great way to start a small business. It's a great small business idea.</p><p>Just visit freelancing platforms like Fiverr and Upwork to see the global demand for designing; the pay is mind-blowing and you get to build a nice clientele list. </p><hr><p><strong>Also read : </strong><a href="https://startuptalky.com/business-ideas-low-investment-high-return/" rel="bookmark">62 Business Ideas With Low Investment</a></p><hr><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "scoopwhoop_CreateAPodcast"> Podcast</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://startuptalky.com/content/images/2020/05/podcast_startuptalky-min.jpg" class="kg-image" alt="top small business ideas for 2020"><figcaption>Create Your Podcast</figcaption></figure><p>Podcasts are booming. They are now seen as the modern form of information sharing and are equal in value to videos and written content. People now actively listen to podcasts. <strong>Joe Rogan podcasts </strong>are highly popular. With over 6 million subscribers and podcasts that are as long as 3 hours, Joe Rogan has become a brand. </p><p>Imagine the number of audio advertisements you can place in your podcasts! If your podcasts eventually gain pace, you can secure brand deals with sponsors like <strong>Squarespace</strong> or <strong>Audible</strong>. They pay a lot to be part of popular podcasts. You can also bring in affiliate marketing. </p><p>Podcasts don't require expensive investment. You can even use your phone's microphone to record an audio file. Upload it to a website like <strong>Anchored.fm </strong>which in turn will share your podcast around the world. Don't know how to do podcasting? Startuptalky will teach you how to <a href="https://startuptalky.com/everything-about-podcasting/">create your own podcast</a>.</p><hr><p><strong>Also read : </strong><a href="https://startuptalky.com/manufacturing-business-ideas/" rel="bookmark">15 Small Scale Manufacturing Business Ideas</a></p><hr><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "scoopwhoop_CreateABlogAndMonetizeIt"> Blog Monetization </h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>Dollar spot is an excellent example of making money through blogs. This blog allows you to contribute posts on topics like banking, how to make money, how to save money, and how to invest. The people working on Dollar spot made three hundred and forty-seven thousand mental dollars blogging this January. Sounds made-up but they have put up revenue reports online for the public to view. This is a easy small business idea to earn money.</p><p>Use AdSense and affiliate marketing to make money from your blog. In fact, market your services through your blog! <a href="https://startuptalky.com/quality-content-guide/">Creating your own blog</a> won't be much difficult if you are passionate about writing.</p><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "scoopwhoop_CreateACourse"> Online Course Creation</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>What's common between Udemy, Coursera, and edX ? They thrive on paid online courses. If you are highly knowledgeable about something, say machine learning, and know to explain complex content lucidly, then creating an online course is a good business idea for you.</p><p>Come up with an agenda and outline what topics you will cover. Then create videos on each of the topics. Make the visually appealing while not going overboard. Have transcriptions ready for the videos. Once all items are taken care of, bundle everything into a package and search for websites that pay well for your course.</p><hr><p><strong>Also read : </strong><a href="https://startuptalky.com/business-development-tools/" rel="bookmark">Best Business Development Tools For More Profit</a></p><hr><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "byju_SellProductsOnline"> Sell Products Online</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>Creating an online store is not difficult today. Shopify is one such platform which allows you to create an online store. Professional templates that are super-easy to customize make it a child's play to sell products online through a store. Drop shipping is another popular term that's related to selling products online.</p><!--kg-card-begin: html--> <h2 id =  "rivigo_DoPrintOnDemand"> Printing On Demand</h2><!--kg-card-end: html--><p>There are situations when the design is ready but there's no way to print it.
