						<p><strong>Advertising</strong> is a crucial part of marketing. <strong>Advertising</strong> is a purpose to inform the consumers about your product. There are many Advertising platforms, Google Ad, LinkedIn and Facebook. Lately <strong>Quora</strong> has also entered this market. <strong>Quora</strong> now offers cheap Ads at Cost per click basis. You can employ <strong>Quora advertising</strong> to reach a bigger audience who are already looking for answers to questions that relate to your business or industry.</p><h2 id="what-is-quora-advertising">What is Quora Advertising?</h2><p>Quora is a self service platform. Any advertiser can run ads on quora. Quora enables advertisers to target specific topics and questions on Quora, as well as create and target audiences based on pixel data (behavioral targeting).</p><h2 id="how-to-advertise-on-quora-in-5-simple-steps">How to Advertise on Quora in 5 Simple steps</h2><h3 id="1-get-started">1. Get started</h3><p>To get started with advertising you need to visit Quora.com/business and Click on Start now. After you click on start now you will be asked to log into your account. </p><p>After you sign in to your account fill in your business details and click on create account. Your Quora advertising account will be created.</p><h3 id="2-create-campaign">2. Create Campaign</h3><p>Once you create your advertising account Ads manager page will be displayed. Now You are all set to Create your first campaign. To create your first ad campaign Click on Create Campaign.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising_Startuptalky--2-.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora advertising" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Create Campaign</strong></figcaption></figure><p>The ads manager page will be displayed. Fill in the details like Name of your campaign, objective, budget and the Schedule. You can either prefer to start your campaign immediately or can set a start and end date. </p><h3 id="3-create-an-ad-set">3. Create an Ad Set</h3><p>Once your done with setting up the campaign, you have to create an Ad set. Ad Set is the Audience your targeting and how much you will bid for it.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising_Startuptalky--3-.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora advertising" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Create Ad Set</strong></figcaption></figure><p>Add a name to your ad set. There are two types of targeting topic targeting and audience targeting. </p><p><strong>Topic targeting-</strong>  You can use topic targeting to reach your target audience with Topics relevant to your business. Topic targeting is widely used for targeting on Quora Ads.</p><p><strong>Audience targeting- </strong>You can use audience targeting to target who or the type of user, that sees your ad on Quora.</p><p>You can choose to show your ads on mobile or desktop. You can also Exclude certain locations where you don't want to target the audience.</p><p>Set a bid on Cost per Click Bid. Cost-per-click (CPC) bidding means that you pay for each click on your ads.</p><hr><figure class="kg-card kg-bookmark-card"><a class="kg-bookmark-container" href="https://startuptalky.com/quora-marketing-guide/"><div class="kg-bookmark-content"><div class="kg-bookmark-title">The Ultimate Guide on using Quora for Marketing | Quora Marketing</div><div class="kg-bookmark-description">Marketing on Quora brings a lot of traffic which converts like crazy. The
Internet is a great place for brands and businesses to connect with their
audience. Social networks. Bookmarking site and discussion forums have greatly
helped us to keep building our brand visibility regular promote goodwill …</div><div class="kg-bookmark-metadata"><img class="kg-bookmark-icon" src="https://startuptalky.com/favicon.png"><span class="kg-bookmark-author">Parnas Ghosh</span><span class="kg-bookmark-publisher">StartupTalky</span></div></div><div class="kg-bookmark-thumbnail"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2019/11/Quora-business-guide-startuptalky.png"></div></a></figure><hr><h3 id="4-create-a-new-ad">4.  Create a new Ad</h3><p>Now you have to create the ad. Add all the details required to create your ad. On the right side you can see your Ad preview. </p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising_Startuptalky--4-.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora advertising" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Create an Ad</strong></figcaption></figure><h3 id="5-review-your-ads">5. Review your Ads</h3><p>Review your Ad and Add the suitable payment method. Once you add the Payment method just hit complete order and your campaign will start. </p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising_Startuptalky--5-.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora advertising" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Review your Ad</strong></figcaption></figure><hr><figure class="kg-card kg-bookmark-card"><a class="kg-bookmark-container" href="https://startuptalky.com/best-storytelling-ads/"><div class="kg-bookmark-content"><div class="kg-bookmark-title">List Of Best Storytelling Ads | Mercedes,Travelers Tops ’Best Storytelling Ads”</div><div class="kg-bookmark-description">We have been telling stories forever and our brains have evolved to love them
eventually because a story tends to make information way more memorable. Now, we
have harnessed the power of storytelling power for marketing. These best
storytelling ads tell not only real-life stories but, historical tal…</div><div class="kg-bookmark-metadata"><img class="kg-bookmark-icon" src="https://startuptalky.com/favicon.png"><span class="kg-bookmark-author">Avantika Bhardwaj</span><span class="kg-bookmark-publisher">StartupTalky</span></div></div><div class="kg-bookmark-thumbnail"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/storytelling-ads_startuptalky.jpg"></div></a></figure><hr><p>This is how your Ad will be displayed on Quora.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising-final_Startuptalky.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Ad Displayed on website</strong></figcaption></figure><p>You can manage and Review yours Ads using Ads manager.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="https://static.startuptalky.com/2020/07/Quora-advertising-Review_Startuptalky.png" class="kg-image" alt="quora advertising" width="800" height="450"><figcaption><strong>Manage your Ads</strong></figcaption></figure><h2 id="quora-advertising-cost">Quora Advertising Cost</h2><p>The advertising cost on quora is different than other advertising platforms. Quora Ads are priced on a cost-per-click (CPC) basis, so the cost you pay for an ad is determined by your CPC bid(highest bids gets priority) and other factors.</p><p>When you first start running ads on Quora, you will be charged when you hit your billing threshold, or on the first day of the following month. Billing threshold is 25$, so as you hit 25$ you will be charged by quora. If your account spends 20$ in a month nothing is charged to the account and you will be charged on the first day of the following month.</p><p>Advertising on Quora is not as effective compared to other advertising platforms, Linked in or Facebook. The cost of the Ads on quora is also much cheaper than other advertising platforms. Quora Ads are priced on a cost-per-click (CPC) basis which is quite affordable than other advertising platforms.</p>
